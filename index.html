What Qavona Actually Is

A marketplace where a Physical store and a Digital store live under the same login — without pretending they're the same business.

Most tools force a choice: sell things you ship, or sell things you deliver instantly — rarely both, without duct-taping two platforms together. Qavona gives you a dedicated store for each, built around how that kind of selling actually works, plus the marketplace traffic, payments, and payout speed to make either one worth running.
